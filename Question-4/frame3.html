EXAMPLES

    Topic 1: Covid-19 and its variants

  1. Alpha Variant: A highly transmissible COVID-19 variant first identified in the United Kingdom.
  2. Vaccination Rollout: Countries globally administering vaccines to curb the virus's spread.
  3. Mutation Rate: SARS-CoV-2's ability to mutate and generate new variants.
  4. Delta Variant Impact: The Delta variant causing surges in cases in various countries.

    Topic 2: India in Covid 19

  1. Oxygen Shortages: India faced shortages of medical oxygen during the second wave.
  2. COVID-19 Testing Centers: Rapid expansion of testing facilities to detect cases.
  3. COVID-19 App: "Aarogya Setu" app for contact tracing and health information.
  4. Vaccination Drives: Mass vaccination campaigns and homegrown vaccines.

    Topic 3: Precautions to be taken in Covid 19

  1. Mask Mandates: Wearing masks in public places and on public transportation.
  2. Social Distancing: Maintaining 6 feet of distance in public spaces.
  3. Hand Sanitizing: Frequent hand sanitizing with at least 60% alcohol content.
  4. Get Vaccinated: Encouraging eligible individuals to get vaccinated.
